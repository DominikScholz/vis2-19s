Real-Time Interactive Satellite Tracker

This application was a student project by Lucas da Cunha Melo and Dominik Scholz for the Visualization 2 lecture at the TU Wien in the summer term of 2019.

It is inspired by the work in stuffin.space but uses Threejs instead of raw WebGL calls. The satellite orbits were calculates using satellite.js and for the orbit body calculation astronomy.js was used.

We want to thank all those library creators for the amazing work they've built. We want to further credit NASA for the texture of earth and ESO/S. Brunier for the milky way panorama.
